GA4 Setup for Small Businesses: A Simple Guide

Turn on GA4, verify data, and track what matters.

1) Create a GA4 property

In Google Analytics, create a GA4 property and get your Measurement ID (e.g., G-XXXXXX). Place it on every page.

2) Core events to track

  • page_view (default)
  • click/call: track tel: link clicks
  • form_submit: submit on contact forms
  • file_download: brochure or PDF

3) Validate with Realtime

Open your site and Realtime in GA4. Click through pages and confirm events fire. Then create basic reports.
