AI Development in the Netherlands: How SMEs Can Start Fast

A practical guide: quick wins, realistic timelines, and investment ranges.

Sep 1, 2024•5 min read

Where AI helps first

  • Automating repetitive admin and reporting
  • Lead scoring and sales routing
  • Customer support assistants and chat
  • Forecasting and demand planning

Timeline & budgets

Pilot projects: 2–4 weeks. Production: 4–12+ weeks. Investment typically starts at a few thousand euros for MVPs.

How to start quickly

  1. Define one business KPI to improve (e.g., response time or conversion rate).
  2. Pick a narrow use case with available data.
  3. Run a 2–4 week pilot with a clear success metric.
  4. Iterate and scale after you prove value.
