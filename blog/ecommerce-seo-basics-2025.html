E-commerce SEO Basics for 2025

Get the fundamentals right to drive organic revenue.

Mar 20, 2025•6 min read

High-impact fundamentals

  • Clean taxonomy: categories -> subcategories -> products
  • Product page essentials: titles, descriptions, FAQs, reviews
  • Performance: image compression, lazy-load, CDN
  • Schema: Product, Breadcrumb, FAQ

Content that converts

Use comparison pages, buyer’s guides, and how-tos to capture search intent earlier.

Measure and iterate

Track revenue by landing page, improve internal linking, and monitor Core Web Vitals.
